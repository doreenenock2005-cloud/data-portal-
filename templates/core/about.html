About the Portal

Building a data-driven smart city through open access to urban information

!

The Problem

City data is scattered across many organizations (data silos). Data exists in different formats (not compatible). Difficult to access, share, and combine data. No central platform for data integration.

Impact:
  • Government lacks accurate insights
  • Developers cannot build data-driven applications
  • Researchers struggle with cross-domain analysis
  • Citizens lack data transparency
i

Background

A smart city relies on data-driven decisions. Dar es Salaam generates data from multiple sectors including Housing, Transport, Health, and Water & Sanitation. Currently, systems are disconnected and siloed.

Need:
  • Centralized data platform
  • Standardized and interoperable data
  • Support for urban planning, research, and innovation
✓

Our Solution

The Open City Data Portal centralizes all city datasets, standardizes data formats, and makes data easily accessible to all stakeholders.

Centralized Repository

All city datasets in one secure, accessible location

Standardized Formats

Consistent data formats enabling easy integration and analysis

Our Mission

To provide transparent, accessible, and standardized urban data that empowers citizens, researchers, and developers to contribute to the development of Dar es Salaam as a smart, sustainable city.

Our Vision

To be the leading open data platform in East Africa, driving innovation, transparency, and evidence-based decision-making in urban development.

Open Access

Publicly available datasets without barriers.

API Access

RESTful API for developers and integration.

Data Visualization

Automatic charts for quick insight.
